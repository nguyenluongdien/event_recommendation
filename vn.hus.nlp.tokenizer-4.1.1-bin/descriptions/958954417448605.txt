- Rex Hotel Saigon. Address: 141 Nguyen Hue Street, District 1, Ho Chi Minh city (the Silver Jubilee).
- Cashew exhibition place: Tanimex-LA, Tan An city, Long An province (40 kms from Ho Chi Minh city).

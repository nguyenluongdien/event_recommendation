Welcome to the Saigon Cypher, a platform for MCs, DJs and Producers in the southern capital of Vietnam

It´s a project with the intention to connect artists, to exchange and to have some good sessions with people who share the same passion for Hip Hop.
MCs can freestyle or spit lyrics, there are no rules...except:

NO Beef

NO Aggressive battle

NO bad vibes

DJs can play beats and show skills with short sets, just let us know before in this group, so that we are prepared.

Producers get the chance to show their work, have different MCs rapping on their tracks and maybe find a good cobination to work with others.

We’re celebrating Australia Day in Saigon – and you’re invited!

It’s on Tuesday, 26 January 2016 and will start at the special time of 5pm at the Pullman Saigon Centre.

There will be an outdoor Aussie BBQ, Coopers Beer from South Australia, Lindeman’s Wines from New South Wales, Australian music and lots more including the live telecast of the Australian Open.

Headline Sponsor is San Ha Foods (Australian Alumni company).
